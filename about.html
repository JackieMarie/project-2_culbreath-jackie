How It Works

Now your car maintenance can be tracked on your mobile device. The app connects with your car's electronic systems. It provides real time and projected owner information. Preventive maintenance saves money!

carGO tracks maintenance. It shows you a dashboard with the current status of your car's functions.

carGO sends you alerts when it is time for maintenance or there is a mechnanical issue.

carGO can help you locate a qualified mechanic. After the car repair, carGO starts tracking again.

Notifications and Email Alerts

You will receive notifications and email alerts when items need maintenance. carGO provides manufacturers recommended maintenance. You will also have a printable log of the vehicle maintenance that can increase the sale or trade-in value of the car!

Locate a Mechanic

We do the search for you in Google maps to send you a list of qualified mechanics with contact information and ratings.
