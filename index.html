carGO

Owner's manuals? Who reads them?! Now you can have your car send you alerts when it is time for maintenance! carGO is a car maintenance application that will read the manual for you. carGO uses your mobile device to communicate to your car computer and relay maintenance and mechanical issues directly back to you. Let carGO monitor your systems and take the worry from you. carGO will even locate qualified mechanics in your area for service and advise the mechanic for you!
